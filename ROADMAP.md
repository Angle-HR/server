## How Roadmap Decisions Are Made

1. **Community feedback** — issues and discussions with the most engagement get prioritised.
2. **Maintainer bandwidth** — we're realistic about what we can ship well.
3. **Alignment with project goals** — features that dilute the project's focus are deprioritised.

This roadmap does not represent binding commitments. Dates and scope may change.
